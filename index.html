Welcome to Euribor graph

This is a small hack that i made because... well... because I wanted to see how much expensive/cheaper my mortage as getting.

So, there you are. A very basic and simple webpage (all javascript) that plots the historic euribor rates (since 1999) up until today and optionally a target series to check if you how much you are fu^d^druined. :)
